About

Blending technology
with visual art.

Jansen Wright is a creative technologist and the founder of JNSN Productions, a visual production company based in Portland, Oregon. Specializing in real-time visual performance, projection mapping, and interactive installations, Jansen brings a unique fusion of technical expertise and artistic vision to every project.

With a background in Digital Media from Ohio University and over a decade of experience performing at major festivals and arena tours across North America, Jansen builds custom media servers in Touchdesigner and Resolume that synchronize visuals with music, lighting, and lasers in real time.

From mapping 40+ projectors across museum installations to leading VJ teams at flagship festivals, Jansen's work transforms spaces into immersive visual experiences.
